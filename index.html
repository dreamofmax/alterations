How It Works

Simple from start
to finish.

We've made the process as seamless as possible — so you can hand over your garment with confidence and collect it transformed.

01
Book a Consultation
Request an appointment online or by phone. We'll confirm your slot within a few hours.
02
Fitting & Assessment
Bring your garments into the studio. We'll take measurements and discuss every detail of what you need.
03
Expert Alteration
Our tailors carry out the work with precision. Turnaround is typically 3–7 days, depending on complexity.
04
Collection & Wear
Return for a final check fitting, collect your garments, and wear them exactly as they were meant to be worn.

The Studio

A quiet space for serious work.

Our studio is a calm, unhurried environment in the heart of Marylebone — designed to give both our tailors and our clients the space they need to work well.

Appointments are kept intimate and unrushed. No queues, no counters, no rushing. Just skilled hands and focused attention on your garment.
